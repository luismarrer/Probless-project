What is Probless?

Probless is a web application designed to optimize the work of technical support teams using artificial intelligence. It simplifies ticket management and problem resolution, eliminating the need for external tools or advanced technical skills, while enhancing accessibility and productivity.

About us

Probless was inspired by the need to make support more efficient across all teams. We saw how time-consuming and repetitive tasks could slow down work, which led us to create a solution that empowers teams to work smarter. With AI-driven suggestions before a ticket is even submitted, Probless offers simple, organized solutions to help small teams and startups streamline support, saving time and allowing them to focus on what truly matters.

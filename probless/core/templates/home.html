Who are we?

We are a team of innovators dedicated to simplifying IT workflows for startups. Probless is a smart, AI-powered platform designed to streamline the process of solving IT issues.

Our AI is designed to assist you in solving your issues before creating a ticket. By providing already used solutions for similar problems and guiding you through troubleshooting steps, we aim to streamline your experience and reduce downtime. Let Probless help you find the answers you need quickly and efficiently!

About us

Our goal is to reduce the frustration and time wasted on handling tickets by providing proactive solutions before a ticket is even created. We were inspired by the potential of AI to resolve IT tickets efficiently and effectively. By leveraging advanced technology, we aim to transform the way IT issues are addressed, ultimately saving time and enhancing productivity for users.
